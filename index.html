Frequently Asked Questions

Is Resume Noir free to use?

Yes. This version runs directly in your browser and does not require an account.

Can I edit my resume later?

Yes. Auto save stores your form information in the same browser until you reset or clear it.

Can I download a PDF?

Yes. Use the PDF button or Print and choose “Save as PDF” for the most reliable result.

What does the QR code do?

It lets a recruiter scan your printed resume and open your portfolio, website, LinkedIn or GitHub link.

Is a profile photo required?

No. Photo use depends on the country, industry and employer.

Can I change templates later?

Yes. You can switch templates, colors and fonts at any time without losing your information.
